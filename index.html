About LABS Foundation

Building Sustainable Livelihoods
Through Innovation

Livelihood Advancement Business School Foundation integrates agricultural science, business training, and environmental stewardship to transform farming communities.

🎓

Farmer Training Programs

Hands-on workshops in modern aquaculture practices, from pond management to species selection and feed optimization.

🔬

Research & Innovation

Developing climate-resilient farming techniques through selective breeding, sustainable feed formulations, and low-carbon rearing systems.

🤝

Market Linkages

Connecting farmers directly with buyers, reducing middleman costs, and ensuring fair prices for sustainably farmed produce.

🌍

Environmental Stewardship

Reducing the carbon footprint of aquaculture through GHG monitoring, conservation guidelines, and eco-friendly practices.

Our Integrated Approach
